About Me


I'm Steve, been an engineer with SKY for over 11 years, became an Specialist Engineer over 7 years ago, loved every minute of it! Below is just a few sections of my favourite interests...




Music

From a very early age I've been a Blink 182 fan! they are the reason I started learning electric & bass guitar, I relate to many of there songs to this day and I cannot wait to see them next year! I'm also into any music with awesome guitar riff, interesting bass lines and rock/punk kinda style music...


Cars

I have a tuned Alfa Red Brera, there are now less than 2500 of these in the UK now! We have spent more money than I want to admit and tuned the suspension, ecu and exhaust, next job is the leaky radiator which I'm gonna have to replace and eventually the peeling paint...


Sports

The sport I follow the most is Formula 1. Working in my region, being the home of Silverstone which is only 1hrs drive away and many of the teams HQ's being close by, I've had a chance to work for many mechanics and have a few friends who worked for Mercedes. During silverstone 2019, I sat at woodcote for practice 2 with many Mercedes Engineers watching Hamilton and Bottas speed past. can you tell im a Mercedes fan yet... WEC Toyota Gazoo Racing & Aston Martin Racing are my other supported teams. I'm also an Aston Villa fan, NHL Ducks wannabe fan and interested in many other sports...


Gaming

I've always been a gamer. My favourite games used to be Call of Duty & Fifa on Playstation and Sonic on Sega. Now I mostly play RPG's,Digital Boardgames (since covid),Racing Sims and a smattering of others on my custom built PC & the Valve Steamdeck! My favourite currently is Cyberpunk 2077. I'm also a Sim Racer! I'm part of a team called TSR, we mostly race endurance series like LeMans and imsa on iRacing which is a realistic simulator used by the likes of F1 driver's Max Verstappen & Lando Norris.
